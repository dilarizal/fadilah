Balanced Diet: Eat a variety of foods from all the food groups; vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day, aiming for at least 8 glasses. Avoid sugary drinks and limit caffeine.

Portion Control: Be mindful of portion sizes to prevent overeating. Eating slowly can help you recognize when you're full

üèÉ Exercise

Consistency is Key: Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity, such as brisk walking, 5 times a week.

Strength Training: Incorporate strength exercises at least twice a week to build muscle and maintain bone health.

Stretching: Stretch daily to improve flexibility, reduce muscle stiffness, and prevent injury.
